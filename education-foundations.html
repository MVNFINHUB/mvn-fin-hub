Saving vs investing

Saving is setting aside money for short-term needs or emergencies (low risk, highly liquid). Investing is allocating money with the expectation of future returns (higher risk, longer horizon).

Quick rule

Keep an emergency buffer (savings) before longer-term investing.

Exercise

List three short-term goals and estimate how much you need in a savings buffer for each (timeframe & amount).
